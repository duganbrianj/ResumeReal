About Me

I am a student at the University of Missouri with majors in Political Science and Marketing. I am also pursuing a minor in Geographic Information Systems and I hope to apply GIS technology to Marketing Information Systems. I am very involved with service within Columbia Missouri and have done over 200 hours of service in the past year. I am also the Recruitment Chair of Mizzou's largest service fraternity, Alpha Phi Omega. I am a Co-Founder of both Lost in the Sauce and Globo. Lost in the Sauce is a specialty sauce store that focuses on selling local, regional, and international sauces. Lost in the Sauce is located inside the Mizzou Student Center. Globo is a global news aggregator that automatically translates news into the users spoken languages. Globo is designed to allow people to break through the American perspective of journalism by eliminating the language barrier.
